PRE-COMPETITION MEDICALS

ProAm, also known as Athlete Advantage Medical, is proudly headquartered in Las Vegas and works with medical professionals, athletes of all levels, sports organizations, and managers across the nation, providing everything from physical exams and labs to EKGs. Through our Medical Treatment Network, we offer a one point of contact for fighter, manager, and promotion to schedule and complete ALL state-required medicals and medical suspension clearances.

ProAm is also an outsourced medicals coordinator. In addition to managing athlete’s medicals, we store records electronically and work directly with athletic commissions to ensure athletes are licensed and medically cleared in time to compete.

We work with all major fight promotions, including UFC, Bellator, PFL, Top Rank, PBC, Matchroom, and Golden Boy, as well as many local and regional promotions throughout the US. ProAm has partnered with only the best medical providers and resources to serve our clients both locally in Las Vegas and across the United States, offering a comprehensive range of exams. We assist promotions in managing the Federal and State licensing process and can coordinate directly with State Athletic Commissions.

COVID-19 TESTING SERVICES

Onsiteable, the COVID testing arm of ProAm, provides complete COVID-19 testing solutions specific to our client’s’ requirements and customized to each event. We are contracted with CLIA-certified, high-complexity labs and medical resources nationwide to provide both onsite and pre-travel at-home COVID-19 testing solutions and protocols to our sports, entertainment, and corporate clients, including UFC, NCAA, Miss Universe, and The Cosmopolitan Hotel and Casino.

Our state-of-the-art technology platform by Gomeyra.com provides our clients with real-time testing verification for access into secured locations and efficient management of results.

For pre-travel at-home testing, ProAm overnight ships either a Rapid Antigen or RT/PCR test kit, schedules and completes testing through ZOOM, return ships to the lab, and provides a report.

Pro Am holds all appropriate licensing and insurance as a medical practice, including CLIA licensing and authorization to conduct COVID testing under the FDA/EUA.

EXPEDITED INJURY TREATMENT

With Expedited Injury Treatment, ProAm has revolutionized the injury treatment process by consolidating evaluations, referrals, and office visits to dramatically reduce the time it takes to receive appropriate care and ensure patients do not “fall through he cracks” of the often complicated and disjointed medical treatment and insurance process. ProAm’s mobile app starts the treatment process at the time of injury, with treatment counselors ready and assigned to oversee each patient’s journey from start to finish.

For uninsured patients, ProAm offers reduced cash pay rates and has negotiated reduced rates within it’s medical provider network because everyone should be able to afford proper medical care.

ProAm provides Physical Therapy treatment for all athletes within its medical facility, specific to injury and sport, ensuring patients are 100% and remain injury free.

“The days of running all around town, waiting for doctor’s appointments, and being treated like a number are over.
